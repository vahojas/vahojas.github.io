About Me

I am a first year PhD student at Caltech in the Applied and Computational Mathematics (ACM) option. My research interests lie in numerical methods for scientific computing.

Before joining Caltech, I completed my MSc at Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile, where I worked with Carlos Pérez-Arancibia and Manuel A. Sánchez on Perfectly Matched Layers (PMLs) for higher-order finite difference schemes.

Publications

V. A. Hojas, C. Pérez-Arancibia, M. A. Sánchez, "Reflectionless discrete perfectly matched layers for higher-order finite difference schemes", SIAM Journal on Scientific Computing 46(5), A3094-A3123, 2024.
